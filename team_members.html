INSTRUCTORS

  • KAILI NIE

    Associate Professor of the College of LifeSciences and Technology, BUCT.

    He got his PhD at Beijing University of Chemical Technology in 2009, supervised by Prof. Tianwei Tan. After that, he started his postdoctoral fellow with Professor Shijie Liu at SUNY-ESF, and investigated on the cellulase utilization. Now, He is focusing on enzymatic modification, synthetic biology, bio-energy, and bio-refinery for high value-added chemicals.

  • LUO LIU

    Associate Professor of the College of Life Sciences and Technology, BUCT.

    He studied technical biology at Institute for Technical Biochemistry, University of Stuttgart, supervised by Prof. Rolf Schmid, and received PhD in 2007. After that, he completed a drug biosynthesis project for PharmaZell GmbH in Germany, from “proof of concept” to pilot plant. He is focusing on synthetic biology, multi enzyme cascade reaction, and biorefinery for producing bio-based chemicals including bioenergy.
